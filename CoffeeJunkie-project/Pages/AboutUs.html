About Us

Since 1969, the aroma of The Coffee Company's hand roasted coffee beans has filled Carlisle Street, and welcomed generations of customers. After 50 years of artisan roasting, we've become an iconic destination for serious coffee and tea drinkers, and dedicated home baristas. In our store, every day, we hand blend and carefully roast, a selection of specialty beans from leading growers worldwide. We offer over 50 single origin, blended and green bean coffees, and over 100 loose-leaf teas and infusions, through our Carlisle Tea label. We are a family run business, dedicated to offering our customers speedy shipping, personalized customer service and expert advice. We believe that everyone should be able to enjoy the freshest artisan coffee and teas, everyday.
